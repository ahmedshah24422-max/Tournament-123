Frequently Asked Questions

All your questions answered here

How to upload videos?

After logging into your account, click the 'Upload' button. Select your video file, add title and description, and click 'Publish'.

How to edit profile?

Go to settings and click on 'Edit Profile' option. You can update your photo, name, bio and other information.

How to create an account?

Click the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage. You can register with your email, phone number or social media account. Your account will be ready after verification.

How to share videos?

Click the share icon below any video. You can share via WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter or direct link.

How to like and comment?

While watching a video, click the heart icon to like. Write your message in the comment box and click 'Post'.

What are the benefits of premium plan?

Premium plan gives you more storage, HD quality, advanced editing tools and priority support.
